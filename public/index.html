Getting started

Here’s how to get rolling:

  • Search for a person or family

    If we know who you're talking about, we'll take you right to them. If not we'll do a keyword search for them.

    For example: Paul

  • Search for one or more keywords

    We'll scan the entire bible and find verses where your keywords appear.

    For example: Baptize

  • Search for a verse or section of scripture

    Enter a scripture refference and we'll bring it up.

    For example: Genesis 1:1-5

  • Mix-n-match any of the above

    Enter any combination of the above and we'll figure it out.

    For example: Noah ark IN Genesis
